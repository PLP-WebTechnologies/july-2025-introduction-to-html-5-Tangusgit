What is Bug Bounty?🤔💭

Imagine getting paid to break into systems—not to steal, but to protect.

That's a bug bounty: a real-world treasure hunt where hackers uncover hidden flaws in websites, apps, and even billion-dollar tech giants—before criminals do.

Rewards & Fun💸🕵️💥

Companies like Netflix, Google and Paypal pay thousands for vulnerabilities

Dating sites💘 like Tinder also have bugs waiting to be uncovered🔎 (and I'm not talking about the one date who bailed on you💔)

In the process you get to learn how big organisations work and might even spark an idea to make you the next Bill Gates😉

How to Start🚀🎯💻

Learn hacking basics and find a platform you're comfortable with such as Kali linux.

After the basics practice finding bugs in sites like Hack the Box that have been made vulnerable just for you.

Once you're comfortable throw yourself into the real world and use your skills to help companies on sites like HackerOne and Intigriti.

NOTE: Every hacker chooses their ending: 💻💰 Bug bounty hall of fame… or 🔥🚓 FBI most wanted.
